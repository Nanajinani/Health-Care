Health care Transformation

“Our goal is clear - a modern Health care, working seamlessly to make a measurable difference in people’s health at country level.”

Our Transformation approach

Health care Transformation is designed around 3 strategic objectives and supported by 7 major areas of work.

First, we are focusing Health care's work on driving impact at country level and embedding our mission and strategy in our day-to-day work and organizational culture. This work includes the workstreams focused on establishing and operationalizing an impact-focused, data-driven strategy and a new results-focused, collaborative culture.

Second, we are establishing a fit-for-purpose organization to deliver our mission and strategy, anchored in new ways of working that are enabled by “best-in-class” processes and an aligned, 3-level operating model.

And third, we are leveraging our partners and the global community to drive health outcomes, including through new partnership and resource mobilization initiatives.

Underpinning all of these objectives is our work to ensure that we have a diverse, motivated and fit-for-purpose workforce.
